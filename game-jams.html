Spectra Hunt

Made in 2 weeks for Acerola Jam 0
Theme : Aberration
Team size : 1

Play in the browser | Download | Source Code

This Jam made me learn to rig and animate quadrupeds in Blender, and create cutscenes with the Unity Timeline. It was an occasion to experiment with shaders and visual effects to create a beautiful low-poly flat-shaded environment.

I am especially proud of the dynamic clouds casting moving shadows on the map, and the custom water shader with planar reflections.

Homework Havoc

Made in 72 hours for Ludum dare 52
Theme : Limited space
Team size : 1

Play in the browser | Source Code

This jam was made with Phaser 3 and Typescript, and was my first time producing my own sound effects. It also had been a while since I produced my own 2d texture assets, dating from Dazel the Gazelle.

The biggest challenge of this jam was creating the stack system for the paper sheets. It was slightly inspired by systems like the one in Papers, Please, but instead of shifting the item you were dragging to the top of the rendering order, you have to slip it through to place it on the top.
